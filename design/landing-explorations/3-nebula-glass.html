Nothing escapes validation.

The server is the gravity well. Clients orbit with their own local SQLite; the commit log keeps everything in order.

Scopes as code

Authorization is a written rule, not a hope. Every commit is checked against scopes before it lands in the log.

Offline outbox

Drift out of contact and keep writing. The outbox replays your mutations, in order, when you re-enter range.

Honest conflicts

Conflicts are surfaced, never silently merged. You decide the resolution; the log records it.

Realtime over WebSocket

Committed writes propagate to live subscribers at 0.2 ms p95. No polling, no drift.

CRDT columns & blobs

Opt into CRDT semantics per column where merge is the right answer. Blobs sync alongside your rows.

Optional E2EE & windowed sync

Encrypt end-to-end when the data demands it. Window the sync so each device carries only what it needs.
