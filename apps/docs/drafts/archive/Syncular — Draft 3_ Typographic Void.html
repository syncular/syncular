What it does

Offline-First

Every mutation persists to local SQLite before syncing. Works without network. Always.

Commit-Log

Git-inspired ordered history. Every change is a commit. Merge with confidence.

Scoped Auth

Row-level security through subscription scopes. Fine-grained by design.

How it feels

Instant

Sub-millisecond queries against local data. No spinners. No loading states.

Type-Safe

Kysely-powered queries with full TypeScript inference. Catch errors at compile time.

Sovereign

Self-hosted. Open source. MIT licensed. Your infrastructure, your rules.
