Navigation Instruments

Everything you need to
navigate the darkness

Like a well-equipped vessel, Syncular gives you the instruments to handle any conditions at sea — from calm waters to the fiercest storms.

The Compass

Conflict resolution & routing. Automatic CRDT-style merging determines the true heading when multiple clients diverge. Your data always knows which way is north.

The Anchor

Local-first persistence. Data is stored on-device first, always. Your app stays grounded and functional even when the network vanishes beneath the waves.

Signal Flags

Sync protocol & messaging. A clean push/pull protocol with commit-log semantics. Scope keys, subscriptions, and realtime WebSocket wake-ups keep every vessel in formation.

The Sextant

Schema & type safety. Precise TypeScript types generated from your schema. End-to-end type safety from database to client — navigate with certainty.

The Ship's Log

Commit-log architecture. Every mutation is an entry in an append-only log. Full audit trail, time-travel debugging, and snapshot chunking for efficient bootstrap.

The Spyglass

Admin console & observability. See every client, every sync operation, every conflict from the lighthouse tower. A built-in console gives you full visibility over your fleet.

Safe Harbor

The voyage home

No matter how far out to sea your clients drift, the beacon guides them back.
Here is how every ship finds safe harbor.

01

Adrift in the fog

The client goes offline — a ship lost in fog. But it does not stop. Local writes continue into the outbox, stored safely on-device. The app works as if nothing happened. Every mutation is an entry in the ship's log, waiting.

status: offline  ·  outbox: 12 pending mutations  ·  local db: consistent
02

The beacon finds you

Network returns — the beam of light cuts through the fog. The WebSocket wake-up fires or the next HTTP poll succeeds. The client sees the lighthouse. It is time to come home.

event: ws:connected  ·  signal: beacon_received
03

Navigating home

The outbox pushes its mutations to the server. The server pulls down any changes from other clients. Conflicts are resolved automatically — every divergent course corrected by the compass of your conflict resolution logic.

push: 12 mutations sent  ·  pull: 8 remote changes merged
04

Safe harbor

All clients converge. The fleet is in formation again — every device consistent, every record accounted for. The outbox is empty. Until the next voyage into the fog.

status: synced  ·  outbox: 0  ·  consistency: converged
